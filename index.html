About

Hi! You have reached my (intermittently updated) personal webpage. I am a Senior Research Engineer at Synthesia. I work on topics in Speech and Natural Language. Before this, I was a Machine Learning Engineer at Roku, and an Applied Scientist at Amazon. I was an Applied Scientist at Amazon, UK. I completed PhD at School of Computing, National University of Singapore, where I was associated with the Web Information Retrieval / Natural Language Processing Group (WING) and was advised by associate professor Dr. Min-Yen Kan. For little under an year, I also had an appointment at Institute for Application of Learning Sciences and Educational Technologies as a Research Assistant.

Prior to joining NUS, I graduated with a B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Patna. I also worked for an year at Cisco Systems as Software Development Engineer. I also spent four months as PhD research intern at Naver Labs Europe (erstwhile Xerox Research Center Europe).
